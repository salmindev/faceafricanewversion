African Safaris and Tours Activities With Customize Tour Packages

Africa is a paradise for those who love scenery and adventure. African safaris offer escapades unlike any other, each holiday awe-inspiring with the African wilderness, stunning landscapes, and superlative wildlife. All African safari destinations are filled with delight and exciting activities.

According To Various Categories, African Safari Is Classified As Under:

Camping Safari: Best for those love the real African Adventure. Watching the games from the camps in some of the thriving National Parks in Africa such as the Ngorongoro Crater, Lake Manyara & Tarangire Park is the ways to fill the real thrill of African Safari. Choose Tanzania Camping Safari that best suits to your budget.

Budget Camping Safari: With tents and boxed lunches this is the most affordable option for a life changing African Safari that is no way inferior to others. It is the simplest way of viewing the African wilderness in a more traditional manner.

Kenya Safari: In any African Safari; Kenya tops the list being adjudged as the best Safari destination in Africa continuously for the 4th time in 2017. From Massai Mara to Amboseli, and from Kilimanjaro to Mount Kenya , it is an exotic land waits to be explored.

Rwanda Safari: The real thrill of the African Safari can be felt when you would encounter the mountain Gorillas in their natural habitats in Rwanda: known as the country of the thousand hills. Book 2 days Rwanda Safari to find these classic apes.

Honeymoon Safari: An isolated African Safari on a Zanzibar beach is a perfect start to your married life. The vast beauty of the over lapping waves on the golden sands of the Indian Ocean combined with the marine rich food is a great way to make your honeymoon unforgettable.

Hot Air Balloon Safari: Take an extraordinary adventure by floating on the vast Savannah of Serengeti in a hot air balloon watching the dotting grazing animal kingdom below on your African Safari. The bush breakfast is awesomely delicious.

Lodge Safari: Stay in the lodges that exist in various National Parks such as Arusha, to Tarangire, Lake Manyara, Ngorongoro Crater, the Serengeti to have the close view of the African wilds on your African Safari.

Luxury Lodge Safari: Imagine feather pillows, soft towels, custom cocktails and delicious four–course meals in a candlelight dining tent far from the hustle. This would be the perfect alternative to unwind after the hectic game viewing.

Private Lodge Safari: You and your group will have the vehicle all to yourself and can expect special, focused attention throughout the entire journey. Enjoy an African Safari at your own pace.
