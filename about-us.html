About Face of Africa Adventures

It is a great pleasure to invite you to discover the magical world of Face of Africa Adventures. We are a local registered company based in Arusha, Tanzania. Since 2010 we have specialized in organizing tours and excursions to different East African nations such as Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda and Rwanda. Through our vast knowledge and well-planned itineraries we pride ourselves on delivering stress free holidays, offering expert advice and cost effective solutionsto our clients throughout.

Face of Africa Adventures was founded by Hamadi Ningaa. As an experienced guide with a successful history in East African tourism and wildlife sectors, his goal is to share Africa’s beauty and deliver unforgettable adventures for every client. We are passionate about Africa and want to share our experiences, knowledge and expertise. Let us create the best possible tailor-made African experience for you!

Why Choose us

If you are planning a trip to Tanzania or anywhere else in East Africa in the near future, you are in luck. Face of Africa Adventures will help you build the ultimate safari experience.

Here at Face of Africa Adventures, we provide custom tours. Unlike most tour companies, Face of Africa Adventures does not limit you to specific dates and programs. Instead, we carefully tailor each vacation to suit your individual needs.

Mission & Vision

Face of Africa Adventures was founded by Hamadi Ningaa, an experienced guide with a successful history in the East Africa tourism and wildlife sectors. His goal is to share Africa’s beauty and deliver unforgettable adventures to each client.

"It stands for protecting and caring for the diverse cultures, as well as their natural environments, whilst optimising on business and economic sustainability." Hamad Ningaa

Face of Africa Adventures aspires to support and communicate a positive impression on Tanzanian tourism, both locally and on the global market.

We believe that the best is yet to come.
