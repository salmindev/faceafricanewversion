Uganda Accommodation - An Exclusive And Luxurious Stay At Uganda

When planning a trip to Uganda, the right accommodations can greatly enhance your travel experience. Known as the "Pearl of Africa," Uganda offers a diverse range of lodging options that ensure comfort and convenience for every traveller, whether embarking on a thrilling safari or exploring its rich cultural heritage.

Recent Trends in Ugandan Accommodations

In recent years, Uganda has seen significant growth in its tourism infrastructure, increasing high-quality accommodations. Many new luxury lodges and eco-friendly resorts have opened, providing modern amenities while promoting sustainable tourism. Properties like Kyaninga Lodge near Fort Portal and Buhoma Lodge in Bwindi are prime examples, offering stunning views and exceptional service.

Diverse Accommodation Options

Uganda boasts a variety of accommodations, from upscale lodges nestled in national parks to cosy guesthouses in vibrant cities. Experience the comfort of luxury safari lodges that provide breathtaking views of wildlife and the stunning landscapes of places like Bwindi Impenetrable National Park and Queen Elizabeth National Park. For a more intimate experience, consider tented camps that blend adventure with comfort, immersing yourself in nature while enjoying modern amenities.

Trackers Safari Lodge

Trackers Safari Lodge is located in Buhoma Sector of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park,

Trackers Safari Lodge is located in Buhoma Sector of Bwindi Impenetrable National Park which is one of Africa’s most diverse forests formed as a part of a much larger forest belt that stretched south to the slopes of the Virunga Mountains until about 500 years ago when agriculturalists encroached on the forest and started planting food. Set on an altitude of 1500m just five minutes’ drive from the park Headquarters Bwindi Impenetrable Forest National Park is home to at least 120 mammal species including its famous residents- the Mountain Gorillas.
