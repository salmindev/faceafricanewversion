Kenya Accommodation - An Exclusive And Luxurious Stay At Kenya

Kenya offers a diverse range of accommodation options, catering to all types of travelers, from budget backpackers to luxury seekers. Visitors can choose from world-class safari lodges, tented camps, beachfront resorts, boutique hotels, and budget-friendly guesthouses. In major cities like Nairobi and Mombasa, upscale hotels provide modern amenities, while in the heart of national parks and conservancies, eco-lodges and exclusive camps offer immersive wildlife experiences. Coastal destinations such as Diani and Watamu boast stunning beachfront resorts, perfect for relaxation. Whether seeking adventure, comfort, or a mix of both, Kenya’s accommodation options ensure a memorable stay.

Mahali Mzuri

Welcome to Mahali Mzuri, Sir Richard Branson’s Kenyan safari camp.

Located in the world-famous Maasai Mara ecosystem in the path of the annual Great Migration, the camp is within the Olare Motorogi Conservancy. With endless opportunities for game viewing, you can sit back and let the wildlife come to you.
