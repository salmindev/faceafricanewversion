8 Days Tanzania Safari

Tanzania is renowned for offering some of the best safari experiences in the world. With its diverse landscapes, abundant wildlife, and iconic national parks, it's a prime destination for those seeking an unforgettable safari adventure. Here's an overview of what you can expect during an 8-day safari in Tanzania.

Day 1: Arusha

Arrival in Arusha, the gateway to many of Tanzania's famous national parks, Depending on your arrival time, you might explore the city, rest, or prepare for the safari.

Day 2-3: Tarangire National Park

Depart for Tarangire National Park, known for its large elephant populations and unique baobab trees.

Game drives to spot elephants, lions, giraffes, and various bird species.

Enjoy the picturesque landscapes and wildlife interactions.

Day 4-5: Ngorongoro Conservation Area

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage site and a natural enclosure for a wide variety of animals.

Game drives in the crater to see the "Big Five" (lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard, and rhino) and other wildlife.

Optional visit to a local Maasai village to learn about their culture.

Day 6-7: Serengeti National Park

Head to the world-famous Serengeti National Park, known for the Great Migration of wildebeest and other ungulates.

Game drives to witness the incredible spectacle of predators hunting during the migration.

Explore different parts of the park to see resident wildlife and diverse ecosystems.

Enjoy a morning game drive in the Serengeti.
