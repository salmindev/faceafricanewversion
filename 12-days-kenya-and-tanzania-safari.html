12 Days Kenya and Tanzania Safari

Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime journey through the breathtaking landscapes and diverse wildlife of Kenya and Tanzania. This 12-day safari combines the best of both countries, offering an unforgettable experience that will leave you in awe of the natural wonders and abundant wildlife. Get ready to witness the iconic Big Five, marvel at the Great Migration, and immerse yourself in the rich cultures of East Africa.

Day 1-2: Nairobi, Kenya

Arrive in Nairobi, Kenya's vibrant capital, Explore the Giraffe Centre and David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust, Overnight stay in Nairobi.

Day 5-6: Maasai Mara National Reserve, Kenya

Travel to the iconic Maasai Mara, known for its incredible density of wildlife and the Great Migration
Witness the awe-inspiring spectacle of wildebeest and zebras crossing the Mara River, Game drives to see lions, cheetahs, and more in their natural habitat.

Day 7-8: Serengeti National Park, Tanzania

Cross the border into Tanzania and enter the legendary Serengeti National Park.
Experience the vastness of the Serengeti and its resident wildlife during game drives.
Search for predators on the prowl and witness the interplay of life on the savannah.

Day 9-10: Ngorongoro Conservation Area, Tanzania

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater, a natural wonder teeming with diverse wildlife.
Game drives in this caldera provide a unique opportunity to spot the Big Five in close proximity.
Visit a local Maasai village to learn about their traditional way of life.

Fly to the exotic island of Zanzibar for a relaxing beach getaway.
Enjoy the stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and historic Stone Town.
Depart from Zanzibar with unforgettable memories of your East African adventure.
