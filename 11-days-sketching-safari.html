11 Days Tanzania Sketching Safari

Welcome to a captivating voyage through the heart of Tanzania, where you can infuse your artistic spirit with the essence of nature. Our Sketching in Safari Tanzania expedition seamlessly fuses wildlife discovery and artistic expression, offering you a canvas to encapsulate the very essence of this awe-inspiring nation. Immerse yourself in the bountiful landscapes, diverse wildlife, and vibrant cultures as you sketch your way through Tanzania.

Day 01: Arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport to Arusha.

Touch down at Kilimanjaro International Airport and transfer to your accommodation in Arusha, Enjoy a welcome dinner and receive a comprehensive safari briefing, Overnight stay in Arusha.

Day 2: Arusha to Tarangire N/Park for game drive and sketching

Begin your day with a visit to a local market, immersing in Tanzanian culture's vivid colors and vitality. Set up for your first sketching session amidst the bustling market. In the afternoon, embark on a guided tour of the Arusha Cultural Heritage Center. Your evening is free to continue working on your sketches or simply unwind, Overnight stay in Arusha.

Day 3: Tarangire to Ngorongoro Crater to Serengeti National Park

Depart for Tarangire National Park, arriving just in time for an afternoon game drive and sketching session, Observe and sketch the rich diversity of wildlife and the breathtaking landscapes, Spend the night at a lodge or camp within the park.

Day 4: Game drive, and Sketching Central to north Serengeti

Delve into a full day of sketching wildlife in Tarangire. Explore the park's unique baobab trees and the thriving elephant population. Wind down in the evening with a sketch review. Overnight stay at your lodge or camp.

Day 5: Full Days Game Drive and Sketching in Serengeti

Proceed with a morning game drive in Tarangire, capturing any final sketches. Depart for the renowned Serengeti National Park. Enjoy en-route game drives and sketching opportunities. Settle into your lodge or camp in the heart of the Serengeti. Reflect on your sketches during an evening of relaxation.

Day 6-8: Game Drive and Sketching back to central Serengeti

Devote three full days to sketching the captivating wildlife and awe-inspiring landscapes of the Serengeti. Bear witness to the Great Migration, one of nature's most magnificent spectacles. Partake in morning and afternoon game drives with intervals for sketching. Evenings are reserved for campfire sessions, sharing your experiences. Spend your nights within the heart of the Serengeti.

Day 9-10: Game Drive and Sketching back to central Serengeti

Conclude your time in the Serengeti with a morning game drive and sketching session. Depart for the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Visit a Maasai village for a cultural immersion and sketching opportunity. Continue to the rim of the Ngorongoro Crater, where panoramic views await. Wind down the day with sketching and relaxation at your lodge.

Day 11: Game Drives and Sketching Ngorongoro Crater

Dedicate a full day to sketching within the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Capture the diverse wildlife, including the Big Five, within your sketches. Enjoy a picnic lunch on the crater floor. Conclude your day with a sketch review and leisure time. Spend the night at your Ngorongoro lodge.

Day 12: Departure

Embark on a morning game drive and sketching session. Return to Arusha. Join us for a farewell lunch and an opportunity to reflect on your safari and sketching journey. Transfer to Kilimanjaro International Airport for your departure.
